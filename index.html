Cognitive Decision-making Mechanism

“Cognitive decision-making mechanism” is the cognitive process and strategy humans use when facing decision-making problems. It’s a crucial foundation for data-driven AI research. Studying and applying this mechanism can advance data-driven methods. This will realize the fusion of mechanism and data in computation.

Integrated Space-air-earth

Starting from the characteristics of the electromagnetic spectrum, we focus on revealing the intelligent control mechanism of the spectrum. We research cognitive and decision-making mechanisms, deep cognition of integrated space-air-earth spectrum, network decision-making of integrated space-air-earth, and efficient semantic transmission of integrated space-air-earth. This forms the theory of machine learning and intelligent control in electromagnetic spectrum space.

The Laboratory of Professor Fuhui Zhou

Fuhui Zhou received the B.S degree in Information Engineering from East China Jiaotong University, Jiangxi, China, in 2010. He started his M. S. 实验室简介

About Us

电磁频谱资源是国家重要战略稀缺资源，电磁频谱空间是第六维作战空间。如何高效利用频谱资源，提升频谱利用效率，实现频谱安全、高效共享至关重要。为此，我们实验室从电磁频谱特性出发，重点揭示频谱智能管控机理，研究认知与决策机理、空天地一体频谱深度认知、空天地一体网络决策、空天地一体语义高效传输等技术，形成电磁频谱空间机器学习理论和智能管控理论，为国际标准提案输出提供支撑，加强国际合作，提高实验室在国内外合作中的影响力。

Research Achievements

Resource allocation based on deep reinforcement learning for wideband cognitive radio networks

A wideband cognitive radio network relying on energy harvesting is studied under a practically non-linear energy harvesting model. In order to efficiently exploit the harvesting energy and enhance the performance of the secondary users (SUs), an intelligent resource allocation scheme based on deep reinforcement learning is proposed.
